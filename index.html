Tic Tac Toe
Tic-tack-Toe allows players to position their marks so that they make a continuous line of three cells vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. An opponent can prevent a win by blocking the completion of the opponent's line. Explore more
Demon Killer
Demon killer is a single-player action game . Players assume the role of a young and brave samurai who embarks on a supernatural journey to face off against the evil demons. Defeat the devil and save the world !
Crazy cabbie
Crazy Cabbie puts you in the driver seat of a car as you try to navigate a long road littered with cars that are in your way. There are no overtaking maneuvers in this game; you have to swerve clear of these cars . Be careful not to hit the vehicles that are in your way. Have fun!
Sassy Snake
Sassy Snake helps player control a long, thin creature, resembling a snake, which roams around on a bordered plane, picking up food (or some other item).Try to avoid hitting its own tail or the edges of the playing area. Unlock the thrill
